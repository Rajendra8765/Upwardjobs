FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

You pay a one-time program fee upfront ($1,000 / $2,000 / $3,000 depending on your plan). The success fee — 10% of your first-year salary — is only due after you successfully land a job. If you don't get placed, you don't pay the success fee.
AIS (AI Interview System) is our proprietary platform that simulates real job interviews using AI. It adapts to your target role and company, tracks your performance, and provides actionable feedback after each session to rapidly improve your interview skills.
Starter plan: up to 80 applications per day. Professional plan: 100+ per day. Elite plan: 130+ per day (priority). All applications are tailored, ATS-optimized, and submitted across multiple portals including LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and direct company portals.
Professional plan includes a partial refund if placement targets aren't met within the support window. Elite plan offers up to 50% of the program fee back under specific conditions. Full terms are provided at enrollment. Starter plan does not include a refund policy.
Yes. You can upgrade from Starter to Professional or Elite at any time during your support window. The upgrade fee is the difference between the two program fees. Contact your dedicated account manager to process the upgrade.
Most of our candidates receive their first interview within 2–3 weeks of onboarding and receive job offers within 6–10 weeks on average. Timeline varies based on experience level, target role, and location, but our team actively works to accelerate this for every candidate.
